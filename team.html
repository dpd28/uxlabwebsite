The User Experience Lab Team

Dr. Millet leads a highly collaborative, interdisciplinary team of researchers in the area of user experience, product design, and data visualization focused on investigating interactions between users and technologies with the goal of better designing systems.

Dr. Barbara Millet, Ph.D.

Barbara Millet, Ph.D. is Director of the University of Miami User Experience Lab and faculty of Cinema & Interactive Media at the School of Communication. Her research focuses on the design of user interfaces and their influences on human performance and engagement.

Deb Pang Davis
